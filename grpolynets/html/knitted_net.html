“The most effective solution for packaging of fresh produce”

  • Sofpac packaging nets are used for packaging of small as well as bigger quantities of fruits and vegetables like Potatoes, Lemons, Apples, Avocados, etc.
  • Despite being light in weight, Sofpac packaging nets are strong for holding products. Their knitted structure protects products having soft skin like oranges and tomatoes.
  • Sofpac packaging nets are available in both machine rolls which can directly be applied on the product, and also in desired pre-cut sizes.
  • Our Sofpac packaging nets are a revolution in packaging of agricultural products to replace traditional packaging like cotton bags, hessian bags, bamboo baskets, etc. and they also offer attractive, sturdier and durable packaging with ventilation.
